## Platform Strategy

### Pinterest (PRIMARY - Highest Priority)
**Why:** 80% female, 25-55 demo, visual search engine, evergreen content
**Goal:** Drive traffic to site via problem-solving pins

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Troubleshooting tip pins (link to guides)
- 25% Material settings infographics
- 15% "Before/After" fix pins
- 10% Community repins

**Posting Cadence:** 5-10 pins/day (can batch schedule)
**Best Times:** 8-11 PM EST (evening crafters), 1-3 PM EST (lunch break)

**Board Strategy:**
1. "Cricut Troubleshooting" - all fix content
2. "Material Settings" - cut settings, heat press temps
3. "Cricut Tips & Tricks" - quick wins
4. "HTV & Iron-On Help" - heat transfer specific
5. "Design Space Fixes" - software issues

---

### Instagram (SECONDARY)
**Why:** Visual platform, good for brand building, reels have discovery potential
**Goal:** Build community, drive profile link clicks

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Carousel tips (educational)
- 30% Reels (quick fixes, satisfying cuts)
- 20% Stories (polls, Q&A, engagement)
- 10% Static posts (quotes, community shoutouts)

**Posting Cadence:** 
- Feed: 1 post/day
- Stories: 3-5/day
- Reels: 3-4/week

**Best Times:** 
- 7-9 AM EST (morning scroll)
- 12-1 PM EST (lunch)
- 7-9 PM EST (evening wind-down)

**Hashtag Strategy (mix of sizes):**
Big (500K+): #cricut #cricutmade #cricutcrafts
Medium (50-500K): #cricuttips #cricutmaker #cricutexplore
Small (10-50K): #cricuthelp #cricuttroubleshooting #cricutproblems
Niche (<10K): #cricutnotcutting #htvpeeling #cricuterror

---

### TikTok (GROWTH OPPORTUNITY)
**Why:** Massive discovery potential, younger demo but growing 35+
**Goal:** Viral reach, brand awareness, drive to other platforms

**Content Mix:**
- 50% Quick fix videos (15-30 sec)
- 30% Satisfying cuts/weeding videos
- 15% "POV: Your Cricut won't cut" relatable content
- 5% Duets/stitches with crafter content

**Posting Cadence:** 1-2 videos/day (consistency > quality early on)
**Best Times:** 
- 7-9 AM EST
- 12-3 PM EST  
- 7-9 PM EST

**Audio Strategy:** Use trending sounds when relevant, original voiceover for tips

### X/Twitter (SUPPORT CHANNEL)
**Why:** Quick engagement, real-time help, SEO signals
**Goal:** Answer questions, build authority, drive traffic

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Tips/quick fixes
- 30% Engaging with Cricut community (replies, quotes)
- 20% Link to new guides
- 10% Industry news/Cricut updates

**Posting Cadence:** 2-3 tweets/day
**Best Times:** 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM EST

### Facebook Page (COMMUNITY HUB)
**Why:** Groups are huge for Cricut, page needed for credibility
**Goal:** Link to groups, drive to website, build trust

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Link posts to guides
- 30% Tips with images
- 20% Polls/questions
- 10% Video content

**Posting Cadence:** 1-2 posts/day
**Best Times:** 1-4 PM EST, 7-9 PM EST
